Beach and Island Tour
Explore Nacpan Beach and Culion Island's Beach and Island Tour Embark on a journey of discovery and relaxation as you explore the stunning beaches and islands of Palawan through our captivating island hopping tours. Whether you're seeking adventure or tranquility, our island hopping tours promise an unforgettable experience, allowing you to immerse yourself in the beauty and charm of Palawan's most breathtaking destinations. Nacpan Beach in El Nido is a must-visit, offering a 4-kilometer stretch of pristine sands surrounded by verdant hills. Relax in hammocks at the foot of these hills or seek shade in coconut groves. Enjoy surfing, kayaking, and paddle boarding activities. Nearby, Calitang Beach can be accessed via a scenic tricycle ride, offering spectacular views and a variety of food options.

Experience the restful vibe of Culion Island, once the site of the world's largest leprosarium. Explore remnants of Spanish settlements, including the old nursery, dormitory, sanitorium, plazas, and other landmarks. Visit Aguila's viewpoint for a panoramic view of the island, accessible by climbing 333 steps. Culion also boasts beaches with powdery white sand and offers island-hopping tours to Ditaytayan Island, Malcapuya Island, and Waling-Waling Island. Immerse yourself in the history and beauty of these stunning destinations with our island and beach tours.
