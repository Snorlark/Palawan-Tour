Travel Guide
Brief Travel Guide for Palawan Here are the 6 basic tips to keep in mind before traveling to Palawan. Tip no. 1
Do research about the tourist spot you are going to visit. Prior knowledge about place makes you more feel home and comfy.

Tip no. 2
Be specific with your airport entry. We do not want you to end up at Puerto Princesa, when your destination is Coron. They are two different places with two different airports.

Tip no. 3
Be aware of the local culture and of the existing environmental laws. Be culture sensitive. Removing or catching of some sea creatures such as starfish is stricly prohibited.

Tip no. 4
Transact only with DOT-accredited tour operators. We don't want to ruin your Palawan vacay. So, please be wary online scammers.

Tip no. 5
Always have a back-up SIM card for another network provider. There are some places that only have one working cellular phone signal. Better, ask your partner tour operator if it's deadspot in the place your about to visit.

Tip no. 6
Ready your video or selfie cam. Instagrammable places await, so, make sure to have it with you your powebank, always.
