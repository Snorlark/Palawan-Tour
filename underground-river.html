Underground River Tour
Explore Puerto Princesa's Underground River No trip to Palawan is complete without a tour of Puerto Princesa's Underground River, considered the world's second-longest subterranean river at 8.2 km long. The river also has the distinction of being in the list of the UNESCO World Heritage Sites and part of the New 7 Wonders of the World. The Puerto Princesa Underground River tour involves riding a paddle boat that will take you to the river's entrance. From there, you’ll be boarding a smaller boat to cruise along a 4.3-km portion of the river, which will give you a view of the river’s pristine waters, stunning limestone cliffs, and magnificent cave formations.

Inside the caves, you’ll see interesting stalactite and stalagmite rock formations, as well as birds, bats, and reptiles that live in the cave. Occasionally, you’ll see fishes and crabs within the river system, too.
