Frequently Asked Questions. Unlock essential holiday planning insights with our FAQ section. Keep scrolling for more valuable tips to enhance your stay in Palawan.

  • The best time to visit Palawan is during the dry season, which typically runs from November to May. The weather is usually sunny and pleasant, making it ideal for outdoor activities, island hopping, and beach relaxation.

  • Some of the top tourist destinations in Palawan include El Nido, known for its stunning limestone cliffs and crystal-clear waters; Coron, famous for its diving spots and shipwrecks; and Puerto Princesa, home to the UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park.

  • You can reach Palawan by flying into one of its main airports: Puerto Princesa International Airport, El Nido Airport, or Francisco B. Reyes Airport in Coron. There are direct flights from Manila and Cebu, as well as some international flights. Alternatively, you can take a ferry from Manila to Coron or Puerto Princesa.

  • Palawan offers a wide range of activities for tourists. You can go island hopping, snorkeling, and diving to explore the rich marine life. Hiking, kayaking, and visiting waterfalls are popular for those who enjoy nature and adventure. Don’t miss the chance to visit the underground river in Puerto Princesa and explore the beautiful beaches and lagoons in El Nido and Coron.
