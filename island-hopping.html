Island Hopping Tour
Explore Coron, El Nido, Honda Bay, and Port Baton's Island Hopping Discover the wonders of Palawan through its captivating island hopping tours. Palawan's island hopping tours promise a journey of discovery and wonder. Explore hidden coves, swim in turquoise waters, and encounter vibrant marine life as you hop from one island to another. Whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure, Palawan's island hopping tours offer something for everyone. Come and experience the magic of Palawan's islands and create memories that will last a lifetime. Start your adventure in Port Barton, a charming coastal town offering easy access to Starfish Island, Exotic Island, Inaladelan Island, and the private German Island, where you can swim with sea turtles or unwind in hammocks. Dive into the emerald waters of the Aquarium Reef and explore the Marine Sanctuary, home to Black Coral and Manta Rays.

Journey to Honda Bay from Puerto Princesa and explore Starfish Island's vibrant marine life, Pandan Island's serene white sand beaches, Cowrie Island's thrilling water sports, and Luli Island's magical appearance and disappearance with the tides.

In Coron, experience the pristine beauty of Kayangan Lake, Twin Lagoon, Siete Pecados, Banol Beach, and CYC Beach while island hopping. Conclude your adventure in El Nido, renowned for its Big Lagoon, Small Lagoon, Secret Lagoon, Seven Commandos Beach, and Snake Island. Experience the best of Palawan's island treasures with these unforgettable island hopping tours. The laid-back vibe of Coron Island makes it a top destination among Palawan tour packages, offering lakes, lagoons, and beaches for fun-loving water adventurers. Island hopping in Coron is a must, with outrigger boats taking you to Kayangan Lake, known as the cleanest lake in the Philippines, and Twin Lagoon, a body of water framed by towering limestone rock formations. Siete Pecados is a snorkeling site teeming with coral reefs, while Banol Beach and CYC Beach offer white sand shores for relaxation.

In El Nido, known for its beautiful beach resorts, majestic cliffs, and abundant marine life, you can explore 45 unspoiled islands and islets through island hopping tours. Visit the Big Lagoon, Small Lagoon, and Secret Lagoon for calm, clear waters perfect for swimming or kayaking. Other attractions include Seven Commandos Beach and Snake Island, famous for its 3-kilometer sandbar. Island hopping tours in El Nido are now categorized using the 7 Wonders of El Nido booking system, ensuring safety with limited guests per attraction. Start your Palawan adventure today and immerse yourself in the beauty of these breathtaking islands.
